About Us

Founded in 2025, Altamira Logistics and Recovery Ltd. is a privately held firm focused on acquiring and managing residential and light commercial properties. We also pursue selective private investment opportunities that align with long-term value creation.

  • Hands-on property management
  • Focused, long-term growth
